The significance of the survey results

According to these charts, we can see that the products of Nature Care still need to be improved. There are not many kinds of products, and they are not targeted to specific skin. This is the point that customers care about. At the same time, it can be seen that customers prefer to use email for communication and prefer whitening and moisturizing products. This result makes us have a clearer goal for the future development of the company and better understand the needs of customers. Be able to give feedback to the enterprise or supplier in time.
